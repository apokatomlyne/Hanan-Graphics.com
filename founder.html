Meet the Founders

Driving creativity and innovation at Hanan Graphics & Media Production

James Benarld CEO & Creative Director

James Bernard is a passionate creative and founder of Hanan Graphics and Media Production, with expertise in graphic design, digital media, and visual storytelling. He blends artistic vision with technical skill to create impactful content across branding, motion graphics, photography, and video production.



Driven by authenticity and excellence, James leads Hanan Graphics with a commitment to helping brands tell stories that connect. His focus on creativity and purpose has positioned the company as a trusted name in the creative industry.

Tychicus Mbeja– Head of Photography & Strategy

Tychicus Mbeja is a skilled photographer and videographer with a strong passion for visual storytelling. As co-founder of Hanan Graphics and Media Production, he leads with creativity and precision, capturing meaningful moments through portrait, commercial, and event content.



With added expertise in data analytics, Tychicus blends art and strategy to produce media that resonates with audiences and drives results—making him a vital part of Hanan Graphics’ growth and vision.

Tomlyne Apoka – web developer & Socials Manager

Tomlyne Apoka is a visionary web developer and co-founder of Hanan Graphics and Media Production. He creates responsive, high-performance websites that fuse creativity with precision—turning ideas into powerful digital experiences that leave a lasting impression.



Driven by authenticity and innovation, Tomlyne builds platforms that are both visually striking and deeply user-focused. His work reflects a perfect balance of design and functionality, helping brands tell their stories in bold, memorable ways.
