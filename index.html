About Hanan

Hanan Graphics and Media Production is a visionary team of creative professionals committed to transforming the media production industry in Kenya and across the globe. With expertise in graphic design, video production, photography, sound engineering, and live broadcast, we deliver compelling visual and audio content that engages audiences and elevates brands. Our approach combines artistic innovation with technical excellence, ensuring every project is crafted to meet the highest standards of quality, creativity, and impact.


Driven by a passion for storytelling and excellence, we collaborate with a wide range of clients—from startups and corporate organizations to NGOs and government institutions. Our mission is to create meaningful content that informs, inspires, and connects. As we grow our presence both locally and internationally, Hanan Graphics and Media Production stays committed to innovation, continually redefining standards in visual communication, sound, and live event coverage.
